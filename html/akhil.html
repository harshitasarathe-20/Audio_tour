Akhil Bhartiya Kalidas Samaroh

Listen Audio


Ujjain, the scene of the Akhil Bhartiya Kalidas Samaroh is viewed as the social and the graceful capital of the nation. This antiquated city of Ujjain is connected to the huge names of Sanskrit writing, for example, Kalidas, Vatsyayana, Bhartrihari other than a few artists and researchers of Hindi like Shivmangal Singh Suman, Prabhakar Machve, Gajanan Madhav Muktibodh, Pandit Surya Narayan Vyas and Balkavi Bairagi, to give some examples.

The refined tenants of Ujjain merit a unique note of a debt of gratitude is in order for safeguarding and in addition supporting the abstract and social legacy of India by methods for the efficient Akhil Bhartiya Kalidas Samaroh. Artistic introductions together with Indian traditional projects and dramatizations are displayed at this Akhil Bhartiya Kalidas Samaroh, Ujjain, India.

Writers, Poets from around the world come together in the march for seven brilliant days of poetry recitation, story reading, and cultural performances. Ujjain is the hometown of many eminent writers and poets like Kalidas, Pandit Surya Narayan Vyas, Balkavi Bairagi, to name a few. The fest is organized to celebrate the work of the notable poet, Kalidas.

Several plays of Kalidas, related to different themes like socio-political and cultural are staged during the event. Here, tourists can also see many stalwarts' performances by the budding writers. The fest is nothing less than a treat for eyes and ears.

Every year in November - December, Akhil Bhartiya Kalidas Samaroh at Ujjain is composed with much ballyhoo. Along these lines, it would bode well to design your visit to this city in the meantime with the goal that you may savor some great social exhibitions at this occasion.

Where: Ujjain
Special Attractions: Poetry Recitation & Story Reading
