Duchess Waterfall

Listen Audio

Also known as 'Jalwataran', the Duchess falls are a perennial stream that flows into a deep pool with a crashing roar, a significant feature of the cascade. The falls actually stream down in three levels which make for a great scene for your cameras. Located in the Satpura range, the falls call for a scenic trek of about 4 kms through the rocks and boulders of the hills. This trek leads you to the base of the first cascade, where the splashes and mists welcome you.

The trek to reach the base of the first cascade of the falls is itself a great adventure. Passing through the thick forests and making your way over the huge rocks will make your stay in the Satpura even more adventurous. And the view from the first base will be the great reward of coming here.
